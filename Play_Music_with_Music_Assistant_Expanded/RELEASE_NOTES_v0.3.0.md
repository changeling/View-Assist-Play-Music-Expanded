Feature release: two new album queue commands. No breaking changes — existing configurations carry over.

### Added
- **Queue Album** — queues an album to play *next* (after the current track), optionally by a named artist. Say "queue the album {album}" (or "… by {artist}"). Mirrors the Queue Song command.
- **Add Album to Queue** — *appends* an album to the end of the queue, optionally by a named artist. Say "add the album {album} to the queue" (or "… by {artist}"). Mirrors the Add Song command.

The English spoken confirmations are exact; the other seven languages are best-effort and feedback from native speakers is welcome.
